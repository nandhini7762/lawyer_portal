Law Categories

Criminal Law

Criminal law involves prosecution by the government of a person for an act that has been classified as a crime.

Property Law

Property law governs the ownership and use of property.

Civil Law

Civil law deals with disputes between individuals or organizations.

Contract Law

Contract law regulates the exchange of promises between parties.

Family Law

Family law covers matters such as marriage, divorce, and child custody.

Constitutional Law

Constitutional law focuses on the interpretation and implementation of a country's constitution.

International Law

International law governs relations between sovereign states and international organizations.

Administrative Law

Administrative law oversees the actions of governmental agencies.

Energy Law

Energy law governs the use of energy resources.

Private Law

Private law deals with relationships between individuals, such as contracts and torts.

Legal Profession

The legal profession encompasses those who practice law, including lawyers and judges.

Military Law

Military law applies to members of the armed forces.

Tort Law

Tort law provides remedies for civil wrongs not arising out of contractual obligations.

Labour Law

Labour law governs the relationship between employers and employees.

Hindu Law

Hindu law governs the legal affairs of Hindu individuals and communities.

Judiciary

The judiciary interprets and applies the law in legal cases.

Legal Aid

Legal aid is assistance given to people who are unable to afford legal representation.
